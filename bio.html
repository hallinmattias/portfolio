Who am I and why did i become a designer?

Since I was a kid, I have always been interested in creating and building stuff. It began with Lego and continued with electrical circuits and woodworking. I did not come as a surprise, to people close to me, when I decided to take on the studies to become an Industrial Design Engineer. During my studies I found a new area interest in design. By studying usability to improve the user experience I understood the importance of designing what the user needs instead of what the user wants. That sparked my interest of UX design.
